Virginia Beach: Virginia's Premier Seaside Destination

About Virginia Beach

  1. Population: Approximately 450,000
  2. Year Incorporated: 1906
  3. Region: Tidewater/Hampton Roads
  4. Classification: Urban/Suburban
  5. Average Income: Virginia Beach's average income is slightly above the state average.

Did You Know?

Virginia Beach is home to a boardwalk that stretches three miles along the oceanfront and is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.
